NUST MISIS  ·  Change management and company lifecycles principles
Key Dimensions
Length
Duration of the full lifecycle.
How long a company journeys
from birth to decline.
Height
Peak value reached at maturity.
Maximum revenues, profits or
market share attained.
Steepness
Rate of growth and decline.
How sharply the company rises
and falls through stages.
Flattening
Breadth of the maturity plateau.
How long the company sustains
its peak before declining.
